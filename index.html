What data we collect

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, business name, and project details you provide voluntarily
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and pages visited (retained for up to 30 days for security purposes)

Legal basis for processing

  • Contact form data: Legitimate interest — to respond to your business enquiry (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR)

  • Optional cookies (analytics): Consent — only collected if you click "Accept all"

How long we keep it

  • Technical/log data: up to 30 days

Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject under the GDPR and the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email info@kgdigital.ie. We will respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie), Ireland's supervisory authority.

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Our commitment

KG Digital is committed to making this website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Measures taken

  • Semantic HTML5 elements used throughout (nav, main, section, footer)
  • All interactive elements are keyboard navigable
  • Colour contrast ratios meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA requirements
  • All images have descriptive alt text or are marked decorative
  • Form fields are labelled and associated with their inputs
  • Skip-to-content link provided for keyboard users
  • ARIA roles and labels used where appropriate

Known limitations

We are continuously improving accessibility. If you encounter any barriers, please contact us and we will address them promptly.

Feedback & contact

If you experience any accessibility issues, please email info@kgdigital.ie and we will respond within 5 working days.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the National Disability Authority (nda.ie) or the Irish Human Rights and Equality Commission (ihrec.ie).
